Who We Are

About Troy Reichlein, CPA, LLC

Troy Reichlein, CPA, LLC is located in downtown Lake Oswego and provides accounting and tax services to individuals and multi-state businesses across Oregon and Washington.

Troy Reichlein has been a licensed CPA since 1996 and established the firm after two years of public accounting experience, assisting thousands of clients with their tax and accounting needs.

A native Oregonian with a Bachelor of Accountancy from the University of San Diego, Troy is also active in the community as a Make-A-Wish Financial Giving Committee member and regular tax guest contributor on KATU Channel 2 AM Northwest.
